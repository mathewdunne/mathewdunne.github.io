Extracurricular Activities

Engineering Competitions

I've been competing in Engineering Design competitions as part of a team of 4 students since starting university in both the Junior Design and Senior Design categories. These competitions involve being given some kind of design challenge that must be completed and submitted along with a presentation discussing the team's work at the end of a given time frame (usually 8-10 hours). There is also typically a list of allowed supplies with budget restrictions. The goal is to come up with the most effective solution to the given challenge that you can within the short time window, which you then demonstrate and present your solution to a panel of judges.

Each University that participates in these competitions holds a competition for just teams from that university. The winners of each category then qualify for the Ontario Engineering Competition (OEC), and the winners of OEC qualify for the Canadian Engineering Competition (CEC), with winners from the other provinces. My team was the first team from the University of Windsor to ever qualify for the national competition level at CEC.
